## **1. Worker (עובד – *Oved*)**

### **Role Description**

The Worker is responsible for faithful labor and service within the community. This role emphasizes diligence, obedience, and integrity in practical tasks entrusted to them.

### **Requirements**

* Reliable in assigned duties
* Willing to labor with humility and consistency
* Physically and mentally capable of completing tasks
* Teachable and responsive to instruction

### **Responsibilities**

* Carry out assigned work with excellence
* Support the needs of the community
* Maintain order and cleanliness in areas of responsibility


---

## **2. Elder (זקן – *Zaken*)**

### **Role Description**

The Elder serves as a guardian of wisdom, order, and moral authority within the community. Elders provide leadership through example, counsel, and righteous judgment.

### **Requirements**

* Proven character and maturity
* Respected by the community
* Knowledge of the Law and traditions
* Impartial and just in decision-making
* Able to counsel with wisdom and patience

### **Responsibilities**

* Uphold preaching within the community
* Provide guidance and counsel to individuals and families
* Help resolve disputes within the community
* Support and oversee workers
* Represent the community in matters of judgment

---

## **3. Sage (חכם – *Ḥakham*)**

### **Role Description**

The Sage is entrusted with the teaching, interpretation, and preservation of wisdom. This role focuses on deep understanding of Scripture, discernment, and instruction.

### **Requirements**

* Deep knowledge of Scripture and tradition
* Disciplined in study and reflection
* Recognized for wisdom and discernment
* Able to teach clearly and faithfully
* Humble before God and the community

### **Responsibilities**

* Teach and interpret Scripture and wisdom
* Advise elders in complex matters
* Preserve doctrinal accuracy and tradition
* Discern spiritual matters and provide insight
